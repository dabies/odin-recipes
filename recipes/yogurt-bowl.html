Yogurt Bowl

Description

This is one of my favorite light lunches. It's easy to make, and incredibly tasty. It is also full of great nutrients, and can be customized to be lower or higer calories depending on the quantity of some of the ingredients. I recommend experimenting with quantities and not just using what I do, as I am a large(6'8, 260lbs) professional athlete with high caloric needs. I will list the exact ingredients that I use for this bowl, as they are clean and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Yogurt (Kirkland Signature organic nonfat or Stonyfield organic whole milk)
  • 2 tbsp Nutzo nut butter
  • handful Organic blueberries
  • 1 cup Simply Elizabeth's granola
  • Raw honey

Steps

  1. Put yogurt in bowl
  2. Layer granola over yogurt
  3. Put nut butter on top of granola
  4. Rinse handful of blueberries and drop in bowl
  5. Drizzle honey over bowl to preference
  6. Enjoy!
